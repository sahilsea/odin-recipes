Game Day Sausage Sanacks

These are Fun, Tasty Sausage Snackes Great for Game Day and Parties

Ingredients

  • 12 ounce ground pork sausage
  • 12 ounce Spicy ground pork sausage
  • 16 ounce (1 jar) processed cheese sause
  • 1 slice (pound) Pumpernickel party bread

Steps

  1. Prehit The boiler
  2. Cook Both Ground pork and Spicy ground pork sausage over medium heat until brown
  3. Drain and Mix the Cheese sause
  4. Arrange pumpernickel bread slices in a single layer on a medium baking sheet and place sausage on each slice
  5. Broil the bread slices 3 to 5 minutes , checking frequently until toasted
  6. Enjoy! the Game Day With Game Day Sausage Sanacks
