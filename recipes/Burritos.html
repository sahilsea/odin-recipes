Burritos

Burritos are Fast and easy to make and they are delicious too, so let's see how to cook Burritos

  • Oil
  • 3/4 pound chorizo sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 green chile pepper
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 flour tortilas (Roti)
  • 1 cup Shredded cheese
  1. Coat a large frying pan with oil
  2. Cook and stir chorizo in the prepared pan over medium-heat until browned
  3. Add onion and chile pepper
  4. Continue cooking, stiring occasionally, until onioni is tender
  5. Beat Eggs in medium bowl and add to chorizo mixture, Reduce heat to medium low continue cooking until you get scrambled eggs
  6. Warm flour tortillas in the microwave for 30 sec
  7. Spoon chorizo-egg mixture into the middle fo each tortilla and top with shredded cheese
  8. Roll up the Burrioto and ENJOY!
